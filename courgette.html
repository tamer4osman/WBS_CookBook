Courgette fries

Ditch the potatoes and try these moreish courgette fries as a snack or side dish. Scatter with parmesan before serving

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
plain flour 2 tbsp
courgettes, cut into 1cm batons 2 pieces
oil for deep frying
parmesan, grated 15g

Directions

  1. Tip the flour into a bowl, drizzle in 2 tbsp water and mix to form a thick batter.
  2. Season with plenty of salt and freshly ground black pepper, then mix in the courgettes.
  3. Pour oil into a pan until it is about 4cm deep, ensuring the pan is no more than two thirds full.
  4. Set over a medium heat until 180C or a flick of batter dropped in sizzles and turns brown within 30 seconds.
  5. Lower half the battered courgettes into the hot oil and fry, turning after 1 min using a slotted spoon, for 5-6 mins until golden.
  6. If they begin to stick together, gently coax them apart with tongs.
  7. Remove from the hot oil and drain on a plate lined with a sheet of kitchen paper.
  8. Repeat with the remaining half, then scatter with the parmesan before serving.
  9. Bon Appetit!
