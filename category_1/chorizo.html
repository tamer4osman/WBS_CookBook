Chorizo & mozzarella gnocchi bake

Upgrade cheesy tomato pasta with gnocchi, chorizo and mozzarella for a comforting bake that makes an excellent midweek meal

Chorizo & mozzarella gnocchi bake

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
olive oil 1 tbsp
onion, finely chopped 1 piece
garlic, crushed 2 cloves
chorizo, diced 120g
tomatoes, chopped 2 x 400g cans
caster sugar 1 tsp
gnocchi, fresh 600g
mozzarella ball, cut into chunks 125g
basil, torn small bunch
green salad to serve

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan over a medium heat.
  2. Fry the onion and garlic for 8-10 mins until soft.
  3. Add the chorizo and fry for 5 mins more.
  4. Tip in the tomatoes and sugar, and season.
  5. Bring to a simmer, then add the gnocchi and cook for 8 mins, stirring often, until soft.
  6. Heat the grill to high.
  7. Stir Â¾ of the mozzarella and most of the basil through the gnocchi.
  8. Divide the mixture between six ovenproof ramekins, or put in one baking dish.
  9. Top with the remaining mozzarella, then grill for 3 mins, or until the cheese is melted and golden.
  10. Season, scatter over the remaining basil and serve with green salad.
  11. Bon Appetit!
