Shrimp soup with coconut milk

This shrimp soup with coconut milk is very aromatic, full-bodied and moderately spicy. This recipe is based on the recipe of the famous Thai soup Tom Yam, but with minor changes in the composition and cooking process.

Shrimp soup with coconut milk

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken broth 2.5 l
"Shrimp (unpeeled)" 400 gr
Red fish 200 gr
Rice 150 gr
Champignons 200 gr
Coconut milk 400 ml
Onions 1 pc
Tomatoes in their own juice jar (400g.)
Ginger 2-3 cm
Garlic 1 clove
Lemongrass 1-2 pcs
Lemon zest 1 tsp
Dried chili pepper 0.5 tsp
Cilantro bunch
Salt
Lime (or lemon) for serving

Instructions

  1. Clean the shrimp from the shells.Add shrimp shells to the finished chicken broth and cook for 15 minutes. Filter.
  2. While the broth is cooking, prepare all the necessary ingredients for the soup.
  3. Put pieces of ginger and garlic in a pan with heated butter. Fry for 5 minutes and remove them from the pan. During this time, the oil will absorb the aromas of ginger and garlic and they are no longer needed.
  4. Put finely chopped onion in a frying pan with butter. Fry it until transparent.
  5. Add mushrooms cut into slices to the onion. Stir and fry for 5 minutes.Add chopped tomatoes in their own juice (or fresh grated). Simmer for a few more minutes.
  6. Boil rice until tender. We will serve it separately with the soup.
  7. Put onion with mushrooms, peeled shrimps and small pieces of fish into a saucepan with broth. Now add the lemongrass, chili and lemon zest.
  8. Pour in coconut milk. Stir and bring to a boil, cook for a few more minutes. Salt to taste.
  9. At the end of cooking, you need to get the lemongrass pieces. It is not eaten, but added only to add flavor to the soup.
  10. Ready shrimp soup with coconut milk is served with rice, cilantro and lime wedges.
