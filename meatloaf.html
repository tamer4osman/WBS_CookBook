Meatloaf

This easy Meatloaf recipe is flavorful and moist, made with basic pantry ingredients and baked with the most delicious meatloaf sauce on top.

Meat Loaf (easily confused with Meatloaf)

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Ground beef 2 lbs
Large eggs 2
Ketchup 4 tbsp
Worcestershire sauce 2 tbsp
Dijon mustard 2 tsp
Garlic powder 1.5 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Freshly ground black pepper 1/2 tsp
Dried sage 1 tsp
Sweet onion (diced) 1/2 cup
Breadcrumbs 1/2 cup
Sauce
Ketchup 1/2 cup
Light brown sugar 6 tbsp
Dijon mustard 2 tsp
Nutmeg (ground) 1/4 tsp

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a standard size loaf pan with cooking spray.
  2. Add all of the meatloaf ingredients to a large bowl and gently mix everything to combine (without over-working the meat). Add the meat mixture to a loaf pan and gently pat into an even layer.
  3. Bake for about 30 minutes. Meanwhile, make the meatloaf sauce by combining all ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Remove meatloaf from oven and spread sauce on top. Return to oven for 10-20 minutes or until cooked through (160 degrees F).
  5. Allow to rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
